What is this person

Hello, I am a mostly organic being that mostly wants to write to mostly entertain you.

I put off writing for years, thinking it would take too long or that the satisfaction from having written would not live up to the hype I had assigned it.

I was wrong!

What a fool!

Writing is wonderful, watching the random symbols imbue meaning in the wet matter inside our skulls is a joy I could not replace. Some of that joy is waiting between the lines, ready to jump into you, and hopefully make you happy.
