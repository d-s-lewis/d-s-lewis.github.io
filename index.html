Shorts of Grey

While my little one sleeps, I try to keep my fingers in the ink, so to write. In doing so, I found myself back in Chromatacia, the land of Shades of Grey by Jasper Fforde.

I plan to write more short stories based here, for now there is only the one - A Counting.

There is a baby!

My little boy was born, and is doing his best to stop me from finishing a novel.

Joke's on him! I enjoy his company as much as I enjoy writing.

He is doing very well. He wriggles, jerks, and grunts like a tiny Eldritch horror trying to burrow into my skull in search for sustenance. Little does he know, the true horror is that my skull is entirely empty.
