Pale White Dot

It has been over 60 years since Earth lost contact with the Voyager-1 probe. A group of misfit grad students jokingly attempt contact, and receive something they could never have expected.

Told through a short series of human stories, Pale White Dot details the events following the arrival of the first pale white dot image, and what it means for humanity.
